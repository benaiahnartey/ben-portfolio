About me

I worked in the Agric industry with Wienco Ghana Ltd's cocoa subdivision (Cocoa Abrabopa) for 3 years (2008 - 2011) as an operations supevisor before moving to Yara Ghana Ltd's fertilizer plant as a packaging contractor. After 2 years as Operations lead for OKO Forests Ltd, I started my farm-to-table business (Amandla Foods) during the COVID 19 lockdown in Ghana. My interests include farming, agribusiness and designing edible landscapes for sustainable communities. I'm currently an EIT at MEST.
